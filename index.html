Projects

Survey Kepuasan Pelanggan Polinema

This system is designed to collect and analyze feedback in order to improve the quality of services at Polinema. Users can fill out surveys based on their roles, while administrators have access to manage and analyze the survey results. The system features role-based login, supporting various user types such as administrators, alumni, lecturers, academic staff, parents, industry partners, and students.

SISTEM SDM JTI

This system is designed to simplify the management of lecture assignments and reduce administrative workload. It provides access for department heads, admin, with various activities, monitor progress, and handle document submissions. Administrators can manage admin activity statistics, while department heads can schedule activities and download assignment reports. The system also includes role-based login and a mobile version for more flexible access, enhancing task management from mobile devices.

SISTEM SDM JTI (Mobile)

The mobile version of this system is available exclusively for lecturers, aimed at helping them receive notifications about upcoming and ongoing activities. In this version, lecturers are assigned roles as either PIC (person in charge) or team members. PICs can add and edit activities and manage team members, while team members can update the progress of ongoing activities.
